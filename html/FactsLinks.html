As of December 6, 2019, the National Interagency Fire Center (updated weekly on Fridays) lists one active large fire in California, and a total of five active large fires in the entire US.
As 2019 comes to a close, the wildfire count for California so far is greater than 8200 , representing more than 17% of the nation's 47,777 wildfires. Last year, 2018, California suffered through 8054 fires, over 95% of which were caused by humans.California's wildfires accounted for nearly 14% of the entire country's total wildfires that year.
